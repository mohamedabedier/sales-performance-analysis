## 📝 Project Overview
An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing regional sales performance, product trends, and revenue metrics. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable business insights, tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) across different regions and product categories.

## 📂 Data Overview
The `sales-performance` dataset contains a comprehensive record of 565 commercial transactions spanning a full calendar year. It captures multi-dimensional sales data, including geographic performance (North, South, East, West regions), personnel metrics (Sales Reps), and product hierarchy (Categories like Electronics, Furniture, and Accessories down to specific items like Laptops and Monitors). Key quantitative metrics include order quantities, unit prices, and total revenue per transaction.

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered
* Which regions are generating the highest total sales and volume?
* What are the top-performing product categories?
* How do sales quantities and revenue trend month-over-month?
* Where are the strategic opportunities to improve underperforming regions or categories?

## 💡 Main Dashboard Highlights & Insights
* **Overall Performance:** The business generated a total of **$252,225** in revenue across **565** total units sold for the year.
* **Category Dominance:** **Electronics** is the undisputed primary revenue driver, accounting for **$177,150** (roughly 70% of total sales), significantly outperforming Furniture ($61,800) and Accessories ($13,275).
* **Regional Leaders:** The **North** region is the most active market, moving 200 units, followed by the East (137 units). The South region lags behind at 101 units.
* **Seasonality & Trends:** Sales peaked significantly in **April** (83 units sold, generating $36,700), indicating a strong Q2 start, while October and November saw the lowest activity (22 and 23 units respectively).

## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
Based on the data analysis, the following actions are recommended:
1. **Bundle Accessories:** Pair high-revenue electronics with low-selling accessories to increase average order value.
2. **Boost the South:** Apply the North region’s successful marketing tactics to help grow the underperforming South region.
3. **Fix Slow Months:** Run targeted flash sales and promotions during historically slow months (like October and November) to keep revenue steady.
